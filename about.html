About Us

Structured for Market Impact.

Our Services Deliver Your "Next" Move.

Since 2004, Haykalah has been a pioneer in Saudi consultancy, merging local expertise with global standards to deliver transformative solutions for manufacturers, businesses in commercial hubs, and KSA's industrial zones.

Mark, Haykalah's Business Consulting and Digital Transformation arm, operates on core values that define and drive our work. We empower businesses to achieve sustainable growth and operational excellence through strategic insights and innovative approaches.

We embrace challenges with a "make it happen Entrepreneurial Spirit," transforming obstacles into opportunities and delivering impactful results for our clients. Trust is the cornerstone of our relationships, earned through clear communication and the fulfillment of every promise with honesty, integrity, and respect. Resourcefulness drives us to craft creative, practical, and tailored solutions that ensure efficiency and success, regardless of complexity.

We remain committed to staying ahead, continuously learning, adapting, and sharing insights on the latest trends, regulations, and technologies to guarantee our clients' transparency, efficiency, and progress.

From rebranding and establishing a robust digital presence to breaking market barriers with cost-effective, tailored solutions, we are your trusted partner in digital transformation. Through scalable services, strategic collaborations, and AI-powered B2B and B2G solutions, Mark delivers the next move your business needs to thrive in today’s evolving landscape.
