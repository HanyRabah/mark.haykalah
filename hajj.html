what we offer?

Choose from a curated selection of high-demand essentials every pilgrim needs — each professionally branded to carry your identity with care and clarity.

Water Bottles

Pilgrims will never forget the comfort of drinking cold water under the summer sun. Offer them a practical gift that refreshes — and represents your brand with impact. Choose from a range of bottle sizes, cap styles, materials (stainless, BPA-free plastic), and colors. Brand it your way

Backpacks

Support their journey with lightweight, durable backpacks designed for ease and functionality. Our models come with padded straps, secure zippers, and minimalist styles perfect for Hajj. Select from multiple materials and sizes.
Add your logo on the front, pocket, or strap — choose embroidery, patching, or screen print.

Caps

Protection from the sun meets lasting brand visibility. Our Hajj-ready caps come in a variety of shapes, colors, and fits — from structured to breathable mesh panels.
Place your logo on the front panel or side, in flat or 3D embroidery — all tailored to your brand style.

Izkar Rings

Help pilgrims stay spiritually grounded with compact digital tasbeeh counters that double as personal keepsakes. Choose from models with counters, compasses, and time displays — all customizable in color and finish.
Add your brand to the ring’s body or tassel

Sun Umbrellas

Protect pilgrims from intense sunlight with lightweight, foldable umbrellas that stand out. Available in a wide range of sizes and colors — durable, wind-resistant, and easy to carry during long rituals.
Your logo can be screen-printed on one or multiple panels for maximum visibility throughout Hajj.

Fan

Relief in every breeze. Handheld or neck-hung fans are essential for summer Hajj season. Offered with multiple battery types, fan speeds, and ergonomic designs — ready for long use.
Customize with your logo at the handle or center hub, using vibrant color printing or engraving.

Prayer Mats

A deeply personal companion for every pilgrim — gift them quality prayer mats that serve in the moment and beyond. Choose from velvet-touch or roll-up models, compact for travel and easy to carry.
Logos can be printed subtly on the corner or woven as a stitched emblem, respecting both purpose and brand visibility.

Towel

Cool down, clean up, and refresh — towels are an everyday essential in Hajj’s intense climate. Offered in quick-dry microfiber, in multiple sizes.
Add your brand name using embroidery or printed labels, ideal for high-visibility during use and photos.
