2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data when you interact with our website or services. This includes:

2.1 Data You Provide Directly:
  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company details when you fill out forms or contact us.
  • Account Information: Login credentials if you create an account on our platform.
  • Payment Information: For service payments, if applicable.
2.2 Automatically Collected Data:
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the site, and interaction with website elements.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: See our Cookie Policy for more details.
2.3 Third-Party Data:
  • Information shared with us by partners, affiliates, or referrals.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide, improve, and customize our services.
  • Communicate with you about inquiries, consultations, or projects.
  • Process payments and manage accounts.
  • Send newsletters or marketing emails (with your explicit consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Under GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Obligation: When data is necessary to fulfill our services or contracts.
  • Legitimate Interest: For operational purposes, fraud prevention, or improving user experience.
  • Consent: For sending marketing emails or newsletters.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory obligations.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers:
  • IT and hosting providers for website functionality.
  • Payment processors to facilitate transactions.
  • Analytics services such as Google Analytics.
5.2 Legal Obligations:
  • To comply with applicable laws or enforceable government requests.
5.3 Business Transfers:
  • In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our assets, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Storage and Security

We take your data's security seriously and employ the following measures:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted during transmission using HTTPS/SSL protocols.
  • Access Control: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or comply with legal obligations.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance website functionality.
  • Analyze user behavior for improved services.
  • Customize your experience.

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for details.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

8.1 Right to Access:
  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
8.2 Right to Rectification:
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten"):
  • Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
8.4 Right to Restrict Processing:
  • Request limitations on how we process your data.
8.5 Right to Data Portability:
  • Request transfer of your data to another service provider.
8.6 Right to Object:
  • Object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes or based on legitimate interests.
8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent:
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@haykalah.com.

If you are located outside Saudi Arabia, your data may be transferred and processed in Saudi Arabia or other countries. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable laws and implement safeguards to protect your data.
